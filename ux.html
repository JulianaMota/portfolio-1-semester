BASIC UX

Report

The assignment consisted of creating a non-existent app that could be relevant to the society in a 4 members group work. For this hand in we had to deliver a report that would consist of explaining our idea, find how and for whom relevant it would be and how we would create this business. So, to create this report we have made a field research through tests; worked with problem formulation and a mission, vision, values to explain the idea; chosen the target audience, personas, and customer journey to define our user and usability; and created a business canvas and communication plan for showing how it will be possible and how we will communicate.

Bussiness Model Canvas

The assignment consisted of Business model canvas based on our App RentScanner created in group work. This Business canvas was made for defining our Partners, Activities, Resources, Value Proposition, Customer Relationship, Customers, Channels, Cost Structure and also Revenue Stream. So, with this canvas help us to understand how to build this project and make a business.

Design Documentation

The assignment consisted of creating a design document based on our report and App, where it would explain how our business ideas could be interpreted in our app design. So, to create this document we have made a design brief; a design program explaining our logo, color schemes and typography; a design guide to demonstrate our icons; pretotypes and prototypes to reveal our app design and functionality and also test reports for future improvements.

Prototype

The assignment consisted of making a prototype of your Application "RentScanner". The idea for this Application was a scanner for all renting properties in Denmark. So, we have created a prototype that would show our app functionalities demonstrating the login, list of houses, map, filters, favorites, settings, and profile.
